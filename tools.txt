tools

Chunking Engine	reduces cognitive stress by breaking work down into smaller, more manageable steps.
Pomodoro/Timer tool	offers set time slots (10–20 minutes) to enhance concentration and output.
Ikigai Motivation Module	motivates the start of tasks by encouraging introspection about personal objectives.
Synthesis Tool	arranges and ranks assignments according to their priority and urgency.
Emotional Stabilisation Module	offers consoling direction and emotional support prior to work completion.
